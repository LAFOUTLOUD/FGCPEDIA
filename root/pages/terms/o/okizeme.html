Okizeme

A subset of fighting game strategy that is played when one character has been knocked down and is at the mercy of the other player.

When a player is knocked down, their options become very limited, while their opponent's options keep all of their options.

Knocking your opponent down puts you at the ultimate advantage by making it easier for you to predict your opponent's next move as a result of their limited options.

The art of putting pressure on a rising or grounded opponent.

Also referred to as the "Wake-Up Game".

History

Meaning "Wake-up Attack".

Derived from the combination of two Japanese words: Okiru (to wake up) and Semeru (to attack)
