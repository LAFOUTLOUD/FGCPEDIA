Juggle

A combo in which one player keeps the other player in the air by continuously hitting them, as if 'juggling' them.

A juggle occurs whenever an airborne character is juggled through the air by an opponent.

This includes when a character is launched into the air, and/if, the character was anti-aired and then kept there as part of a combo.

Because of Gravity, a juggle will inevitably end.

In some games, a juggle can be maintained indefinitely, despite gravity, by specific moves and attacks.

The act of keeping the opponent in a juggle may be referred to as 'juggling'.

The state of a character being able to recieve damage while airborne may be referred to as the 'Juggle State'.

History

The first appearance of a Juggle was Mortal Kombat (1992).

Prior to this, airborne opponents became immune to harm after taking damage.
